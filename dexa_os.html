Dexa OS (Initial version)
  • Visual design
  • Icon design
  • Interaction design (coming soon)

Designed to combat doom scrolling, this project promotes intentional device use to boost focus, well-being, and productivity. Each app is viewed as a “Block” in Dexa OS, similar to a widget. Users can keep a maximum of 7 blocks at a time—encouraging intentional and focused phone use. The below screenshots show apps simplified to essential features for a minimal, focused experience—like WhatsApp with only chats and calls, excluding Status and Channels, and Spotify with playback, library, and search, without the homepage to avoid infinite scrolling. Social media apps are intentionally excluded from this OS to minimize distractions.
